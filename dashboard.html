Our mission

Raising standards across
the private rented sector

To make legal compliance accessible, affordable, and verifiable for every private landlord in England — raising standards for landlords, agents, and tenants alike through technology, expertise, and the belief that order and compliance benefit everyone.

The private rented sector houses 11 million people in England. The legislation governing it is complex, constantly evolving, and often poorly understood. Landlords face genuine consequences for non-compliance. Tenants deserve the protections the law provides. Letting agents need efficient, defensible processes.

CompliantUK sits at the intersection of all three. We automate the compliance processes that landlords struggle with, give tenants confidence that their rights are being respected, and give agents a reliable, scalable tool they can trust.

The founder

12 years. Three verticals.
One clear purpose.

Huseyin Turkay has spent 12 years working across three distinct areas of the property market — giving him a perspective on compliance that few in the industry can match.

He has worked in lettings, property acquisitions, and supported housing management — roles that required not just compliance knowledge but practical application of the Housing Health and Safety Rating System (HHSRS), Awaab's Law, damp and mould assessment, and the complex intersection of housing regulation and tenant welfare.

Most recently, as Property Acquisitions Manager at Nacro Housing in the West Midlands, Huseyin delivered 20% above target while managing a portfolio of compliance-critical properties on behalf of some of England's most vulnerable tenants. It was this experience — and the recognition of how poorly compliance tools serve the majority of landlords — that led directly to CompliantUK.

"I've seen compliance failures cause real harm — to tenants who didn't know their rights, to landlords who didn't understand their obligations, and to agents caught between the two. CompliantUK exists to close that gap."
