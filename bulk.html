FAQ

Questions about bulk pricing

Everything agents and portfolio landlords ask before signing up.

Do I need to set up a monthly subscription to use Portfolio pricing? ▼
No. Portfolio (2–10) and Agency Lite (11–50) plans are one-off payments — you pay per batch, not per month. Only Agency Pro (51+ properties) is a monthly retainer. This means smaller landlords and agents can pay once and be done.
What format does the spreadsheet need to be in? ▼
We support Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV files. Your spreadsheet needs three columns: Property Address, Tenant Name, and Tenant Email. Download our template to get started instantly — it takes seconds to fill in.
Can I manage properties for multiple landlord clients? ▼
Yes — Agency Lite and Agency Pro both support multi-landlord management. You can organise properties by landlord client, generate separate certificate packs per client, and optionally white-label the certificates with your agency branding.
What happens if a tenant email bounces? ▼
Our system flags any email delivery failures immediately in your dashboard. You can update the email address and re-send with a single click. The original delivery attempt is also logged in case it is needed for your records.
Can I get white-labelled certificates with my agency branding? ▼
Yes — white-label certificates are available on Agency Lite and Agency Pro plans. The proof-of-service certificate can include your agency name and logo. Contact us to set this up.
Is there a discount for larger portfolios? ▼
The pricing tiers already reflect volume discounts — from £49 per property down to £25. For very large portfolios (200+ properties) or national agents, contact us directly to discuss bespoke pricing arrangements.
