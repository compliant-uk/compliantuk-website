About CompliantUK

Built from inside
the industry.

CompliantUK was founded by property professionals who have spent years working at every level of the private rented sector — and who grew tired of watching avoidable compliance failures harm landlords and tenants alike.

Our mission

Raising standards across
the private rented sector

To make legal compliance accessible, affordable, and verifiable for every private landlord in England — raising standards for landlords, agents, and tenants alike through technology, expertise, and the conviction that order and compliance are good for everyone.

The private rented sector houses over 11 million people in England. The legislation governing it is complex, constantly evolving, and widely misunderstood. Landlords face real consequences for non-compliance. Tenants deserve the protections the law provides. Letting agents need reliable, defensible processes they can trust.

CompliantUK was built to serve all three — automating the compliance processes that landlords struggle with, giving tenants confidence that their rights are being respected, and giving agents a scalable tool that works every time.

Why we exist

Born from direct
experience of the sector.

CompliantUK was not built in a boardroom or by a technology company looking for a market. It was built by people who have worked directly in the private rented sector — across lettings, acquisitions, and some of the most compliance-intensive housing environments in England.

We have seen compliance failures from every angle — tenants who did not know their rights, landlords who did not understand their obligations, and the gap between the two that too often goes unaddressed.

It was that direct experience — spanning the full spectrum of the private rented sector — and the recognition of how poorly existing tools serve the majority of landlords, that led to CompliantUK being formed. Not to add complexity, but to remove it. Not to overwhelm landlords with legal detail, but to give them exactly what they need to be protected.

"Good compliance isn't bureaucracy. It's how a well-run sector looks after everyone in it."

The Renters Rights Act 2025 represents the most significant change to the private rented sector in a generation. For landlords who engage with it properly, it is manageable. For those who ignore it, the consequences are serious. CompliantUK exists to make sure every landlord has a clear, simple path to compliance — regardless of their portfolio size or technical knowledge.
